1. cut 6 35 x 27.5 pieces of 4 mm JAL plywood
2. place a piece inside the laser cutter
3. open glowforge and upload a board design
4. on the workspace, put the design onto the wooden board 
   and make sure everything fits. 
   Lines turn orange once it could be cut. Leave a small margin 
   for all cuts.
5. on the left panel, make sure the sequence says CUT.
